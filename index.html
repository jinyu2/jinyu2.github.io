About Me

Jin Yu serves as a part-time lecturer at the Khoury College of Computer Sciences, Northeastern University. He holds a doctorate in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Stevens Institute of Technology, along with a bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering from Wuhan University, China. Jin's professional journey includes his role as the Director of Machine Learning at Philips/Signify Research, where he focused on driving business growth through AI, fostering public-private partnerships, and enhancing the AI intellectual property portfolio. He is currently the Head of AI at Teradyne, a company specializing in semiconductor testing.
